About us

Founded in 1979, Cudbury Dairy Milk has been committed to delivering the freshest and highest quality dairy products. Our journey began in the heart of Patna, where our love for cows and dedication to sustainable farming practices took root.

Our Mission

At Cudbury Dairy Milk, our mission is to provide nutritious and delicious dairy products while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability.

Quality Assurance

We take pride in our rigorous quality control processes. From farm to bottle, every step is meticulously monitored to ensure that you receive the best milk possible.

Our Products

We offer a wide range of dairy products including whole milk, low-fat milk, yogurt, cheese, and more, all made with love and care.

Community Engagement

Cudbury Dairy Milk believes in giving back to the community. We actively participate in local events and support initiatives that promote health and wellness.
